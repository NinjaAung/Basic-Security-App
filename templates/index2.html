Secure

You know a website is secure right of the bat if its has a padlock! Or if you want to look closer if there is a S in the HTTPS, which means secure. HTTPS uses the SSL/TLS protocol to provide a secure connection, which is both encrypted and authenticated. Information processed here is fine

Not Secure

If your website has a S not a padlock, you may be in trouble as your connection is not secure. Lack ofs eccurity means that information is being processed raw, or without encryption. Your data gbeing process through the site can be stolen
